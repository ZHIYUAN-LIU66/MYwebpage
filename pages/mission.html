Mission

“My Trip, Your Trip” is a personal website offering my travel experiences to people who hope to know about me, and search for some interesting or useful traveling suggestions for building their own travel plan. Unlike other personal sites, which only share their experience but no suggestions to visitors, “My Trip, Your Trip” will not only share my travel experience with images, but also suggestions with links to useful information (e.g. links to the websites of the restaurant or hotel shown in the images).
